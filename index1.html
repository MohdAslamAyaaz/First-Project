What is HTML?

HTML.or Hyper Text Markup Language. is not a scripting; it's a markup language!

Top 3 Fun Facts About HTML:

  1. HTML was created by Tim Berners-Lee in 1991.
  2. HTML is the skeleton of every webpage you see on the internet.
  3. HTML5 introduced the <article> and <section> tags for batter semantics.
